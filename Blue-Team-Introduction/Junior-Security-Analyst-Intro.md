# Junior Security Analyst Intro

**Section:** Blue Team Introduction  

**Description:**  
This room introduces the role and responsibilities of a Junior Security Analyst
within a Security Operations Center (SOC). It covers the basics of monitoring,
alert triage, incident handling, and understanding how blue teams defend
organizations against cyber threats.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- Role of a SOC analyst
- Alert monitoring and triage process
- Incident response fundamentals
- Importance of documentation and escalation
- Blue team vs Red team responsibilities
