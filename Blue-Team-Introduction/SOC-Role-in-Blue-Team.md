# SOC Role in Blue Team

**Section:** Blue Team Introduction  

**Description:**  
This room explains the role of a Security Operations Center (SOC)
within a blue team environment. It covers how SOC teams monitor
security alerts, investigate suspicious activities, respond to
incidents, and protect organizational assets.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- Purpose of a SOC
- Continuous security monitoring
- Alert investigation process
- Threat detection and response
- Importance of teamwork and communication
