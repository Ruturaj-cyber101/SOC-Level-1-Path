# Humans as Attack Vectors

**Section:** Blue Team Introduction  

**Description:**  
This room explores how humans can become attack vectors in cyber
security incidents. It focuses on social engineering techniques
used by attackers to manipulate individuals into revealing sensitive
information or performing unsafe actions.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- Social engineering attacks
- Phishing and spear phishing
- Insider threats
- Human error in security breaches
- Importance of security awareness training
