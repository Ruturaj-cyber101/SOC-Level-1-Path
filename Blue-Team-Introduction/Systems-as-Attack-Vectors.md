# Systems as Attack Vectors

**Section:** Blue Team Introduction  

**Description:**  
This room explains how systems and technical infrastructure can be
used as attack vectors by threat actors. It highlights vulnerabilities
in operating systems, applications, networks, and misconfigurations
that attackers exploit to gain unauthorized access.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- System vulnerabilities
- Misconfigurations
- Exploitable services and ports
- Patch management importance
- Defense-in-depth strategy
