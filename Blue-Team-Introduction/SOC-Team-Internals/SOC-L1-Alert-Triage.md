# SOC L1 Alert Triage

**Section:** SOC Team Internals  

**Description:**  
This room focuses on the responsibilities of a Level 1 (L1) SOC Analyst
during the alert triage process. It explains how analysts review security
alerts, validate potential threats, eliminate false positives, and escalate
incidents when necessary.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- Alert validation process
- Identifying false positives
- Investigating suspicious activity
- Escalation to L2/L3 analysts
- Importance of accurate documentation
