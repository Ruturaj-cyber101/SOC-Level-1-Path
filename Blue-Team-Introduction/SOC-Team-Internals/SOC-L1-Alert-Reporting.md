# SOC L1 Alert Reporting

**Section:** SOC Team Internals  

**Description:**  
This room explains how a Level 1 SOC Analyst documents and reports
security alerts after investigation. It focuses on writing clear,
structured, and professional incident reports for escalation and
management review.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- Structure of an incident report
- Writing clear and concise findings
- Including evidence and timestamps
- Impact assessment
- Escalation recommendations
