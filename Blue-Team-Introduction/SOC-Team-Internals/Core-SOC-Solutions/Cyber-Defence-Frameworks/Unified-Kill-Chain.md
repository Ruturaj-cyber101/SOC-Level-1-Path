# Unified Kill Chain

**Section:** Cyber Defence Frameworks  

**Description:**  
This room introduces the Unified Kill Chain model, which expands
on the traditional Cyber Kill Chain by combining multiple attack
frameworks. It provides a more detailed view of attacker behaviour
across the entire intrusion lifecycle.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- What is the Unified Kill Chain
- Differences from the traditional Kill Chain
- Initial Foothold phase
- Network Propagation
- Privilege Escalation
- Persistence
- Lateral Movement
- Data Exfiltration
- Defense-in-depth strategy
