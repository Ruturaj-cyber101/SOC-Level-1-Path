# Pyramid of Pain

**Section:** Cyber Defence Frameworks  

**Description:**  
This room explains the Pyramid of Pain model, which helps defenders
understand how difficult it is for attackers to change different
types of indicators. It highlights why focusing on higher-level
indicators causes more disruption to adversaries.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- What is the Pyramid of Pain
- Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)
- Hash values
- IP addresses
- Domain names
- Network artifacts
- Tools
- Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs)
- Why detecting TTPs causes maximum pain to attackers
