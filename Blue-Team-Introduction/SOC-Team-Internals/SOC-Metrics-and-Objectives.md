# SOC Metrics and Objectives

**Section:** SOC Team Internals  

**Description:**  
This room explains the key performance metrics and objectives used
within a Security Operations Center (SOC). It focuses on how SOC
teams measure effectiveness, response time, and overall security
posture improvement.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- Mean Time to Detect (MTTD)
- Mean Time to Respond (MTTR)
- Service Level Agreements (SLAs)
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Continuous improvement in SOC operations
